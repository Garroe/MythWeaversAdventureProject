Our history begins here...

MythWeavers started as a stage pirate show, which turned into a bunch of people following us around at a festival. Why? Because we refused to break character. We kept interacting with people OFF the stage. Essentially, we stole the show by having fun immersing ourselves into our own characters. Many of our actors have been speakers and life coaches with previous successful careers. We realized in our coaching events that immersive and game-like experiences offered some of the most transformational value for attendees.
So we thought to ourselves –

What if we combined an immersive experience with of characters, environment and storytelling… with life coaching? What if people came for a fun experience, learned very deep and powerful lessons about themselves and the world, and went home completely different people?
It’s like mixing a theme park experience with personal development. And guess what? It works like a charm. Not only do people love our events, they can’t stop talking about how cool they are.

Get Coaching. Get Results.

MythWeavers offers a one of a kind experience, which is not offered anywhere else in the world. We immerse attendees and clients into very specific story settings, where they need to physically act out and speak out the solutions to the problems and challenges they face. And because lessons are not taught – they are CAUGHT… we have the ability to teach lessons that no life coach ever could, alone on a stage.
