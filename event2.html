Saving Carolina

September 13th 2019 Snowberry Inn, Eden UT

Sunshine calls in a panic because her friend, Caroline, is trapped and can’t get free. Sunshine believes that her friend has been kidnapped and she needs your help to save her friend. Sunshine and Caroline are supposed to be attending this party along with everyone else at the Snowberry Inn. So when Sunshine receives this eerie voice mail from Caroline she immediately runs out looking for her friend. After hours of searching on her own, she calls the Snowberry Inn hoping that her friend is there. Everyone in the room is suspected of being part of the kidnapping and it will take everyone’s skills to solve the mystery and to ultimately Save Caroline before it’s too late.

The Wild West Mystery Weekend

November 15th to 17th 2019 Snowberry Inn, Eden UT

Somethings awry at the Snowberry Inn and mayhem is afoot! Immerse yourself in a wild west mystery unlike any other and spend a fun filled weekend in Eden. Someone has been spooking the locals and the town is in an uproar. The sheriff won’t let anyone leave until he gets to the bottom of it. You will need all your wits and cunning to figure out what’s really going on. The inn is crawling with characters who all have a motive, it’s up to YOU to figure out who the culprit is and why they are causes these shenanigans. Space is limited to the first 15 people to sign up. So, book your spot now.
